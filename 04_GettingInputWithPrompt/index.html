Typically, information is gathered from users in HTML forms. An advantage of using forms is that the user isn't bombarded with a pop-up prompt. Pop-ups being the scourge of the Internet, many users might just exit them without reading the prompt. A prompt might be useful when really trying to get the user's attention instead of just filling out typical form information.
